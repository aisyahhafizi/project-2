About Our Project

This project explores how technology can be used to promote diversity and inclusivity. Our tagline "Code the Future. Include Everyone." , aim to inspire others to see technology not only as innovation, but also as a tool for equity and social good. Through this website, we share our ideas and resources such as the Implicit Bias Test and intersectionality as well as our tech heroes that create the awareness through digital empowerment.
