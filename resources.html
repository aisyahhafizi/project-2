Key Concepts

What is Implicit Bias
Implicit Bias is defined by the Oxford dictionary: unconscious favoritism toward or prejudice against people of a particular ethnicity, gender, or social group that influences one's actions or perceptions.
Implicit Bias Test (IBT)
The Implicit Bias Test (IBT) is a tool created by psychologists at Harvard to help highlight and uncover biases. This test isn't definite but it can spark reflection and conversations.

Take the test here

Intersectionality
Intersectionality as defined by the Oxford dictionary: the interconnected nature of social categorizations such as race, class, and gender as they apply to a given individual or group, regarded as creating overlapping and interdependent systems of discrimination or disadvantage.
