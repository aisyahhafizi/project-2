    • What is Implicit Bias

    Implicit Bias as definied by the Oxford dictionary: unconscious favoritism toward or prejudice against people of a particular ethnicity, gender, or social group that influences one's actions or perceptions.

    Implicit Bias Test (IBT)

    The Implicit Bias Test (IBT) is a tool created by psychologists at Harvard to help highlight and uncover biases. This test isnt definite but it can spark reflection and converstations.

    Take the test here

    Intersectionality

    Intersectionality as definied by the Oxford dictionary: the interconnected nature of social categorizations such as race, class, and gender as they apply to a given individual or group, regarded as creating overlapping and interdependent systems of discrimination or disadvantage.
